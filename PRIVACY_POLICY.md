# Privacy Policy for Dailygraph

**Last updated: March 28, 2026**

## Overview

Dailygraph is a local journal and diary application for Android. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data. The short version: **it doesn't collect any**.

---

## Data Collection

**Dailygraph does not collect any personal data.**

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any information about you or your device — ever.

---

## How Your Data Is Stored

All journal entries, notes, and any other content you create within Dailygraph are stored **exclusively on your device** using a local database (Room/SQLite). Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it yourself (e.g., using the built-in backup/export feature).

- No data is sent to any server.
- No data is sent to any third party.
- No cloud sync or remote storage is used.
- No account or sign-in is required.

---

## Third-Party Services

Dailygraph does **not** integrate any third-party services, including:

- No analytics (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- No crash reporting services (e.g., Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
- No advertising networks
- No tracking or profiling tools
- No social media SDKs

---

## Permissions

Dailygraph requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality. No permissions are used to access personal data beyond what is required for the app to work locally on your device.

---

## Data Security

Because all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, your journal entries are as secure as your device itself. We recommend using your device's built-in screen lock and encryption features to protect your personal content.

---

## Children's Privacy

Dailygraph does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all ages in this regard.

---

## Open Source

Dailygraph is free and open-source software. You can review the full source code to verify these claims independently.

- **Play Store ID:** `com.dailygraph.app`

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated in the future, the new version will be posted in this repository with an updated "Last updated" date. Since the app does not collect data, any future changes are expected to be minimal.
